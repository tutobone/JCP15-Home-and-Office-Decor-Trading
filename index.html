PRODUCTS

KOREAN BLINDS

JCP15 Home and Office Decor Trading imports only the highest quality components from China, and it is fabricated here in the Philippines, guaranteeing the quality of its products from manufacturing and assembly to installation and after-sales service. The two most common types of blinds that JCP15 Home and Office Decor Trading offers are combo blinds and roller blinds. Both come in a variety of materials, designs, and patterns. Combi blinds come in single or dual color options and alternate textiles to regulate the amount of light that enters the space. Conversely, roller blinds are typically used in restaurants like Jollibee, McDonald's, and others. They can be either sunscreen or block out, with different levels of glare rejection. In addition to being manually operated, both can be motorized using a wall-mounted control or a wireless remote. Subsequently, there are vertical blinds designed for windows that measure from floor plan to the desired height.
